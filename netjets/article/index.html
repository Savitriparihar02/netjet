ANNOUNCING OUR NEW MULTIYEAR PARTNERSHIP

APRIL 2024

As the global leader in private aviation, we are proud to announce our exclusive partnership with The R&A as their Official Private Jet Provider for The Open. The multiyear agreement allows NetJets to invite our Owners to the prestigious event.
NetJets has long-standing ties to golf, and the new partnership strengthens our commitment to the sport in Europe. It will commence ahead of The Open taking place at Royal Troon, Scotland, July 14-21. This partnership reinforces NetJets as the leading jet provider for exclusive golf experiences—granting Owners unparalleled access to the world’s most prestigious tournaments.

“This partnership will allow NetJets to continue offering access to some of the most exclusive events worldwide; one of the many benefits our Owners get from flying with NetJets. A brand rich in history such as The R&A aligns with the impeccable reputation NetJets has, thereby cementing a perfect partnership.”
- Patrick Gallagher, President of Sales, Marketing & Service

NETJETS AMBASSADOR BRIAN HARMAN

A MESSAGE FROM THE 2023 CHAMPION GOLFER

With The Open showcasing the world’s most talented golfers, many eyes will be on the winner of last year’s tournament, NetJets Brand Ambassador Brian Harman, during this summer’s event.
Harman’s immense amount of skill, attention to detail and being one of the few left-handed players in the sport has made him a standout in professional tournaments. Hailing from Savannah, Georgia, Harman’s talent has seen him secure several top-10 finishes in various PGA TOUR events, showcasing his consistency, competitiveness, and passion for the sport. Watch the video above to see what he is looking forward to the most with this year’s tournament.

“NetJets has a renowned reputation worldwide for its leadership in aviation, and its long-standing support for golf aligns with our own vision of ensuring that the sport is thriving in the future.”
- Neil Armit, Chief Commercial Officer at The R&A

OUR LONG-STANDING RELATIONSHIP WITH GOLF

A PERFECT PAIRING

Over 50 professional golfers choose NetJets year after year—including eight of the last ten Open champions. Some of the world’s best golfers choose and stay with NetJets because what we offer is truly second to none. We provide anytime access to the finest state-of-the-art fleet, and our exceptional service includes taking care of every detail, so athletes can fully relax and feel prepared for their next sporting event. Adding an additional layer of security, our flights are identifiable only by tail numbers, which are not publicly associated with Owner names. This provides golf professionals with the much-needed privacy they expect and deserve.
